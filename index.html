About Me

Piper Starnes is a music and culture writer from Tega Cay, South Carolina.
In addition to feature stories and critical reviews, she enjoys writing quirky, data-driven pieces about entertainment and history.
Her specialties are classical music, K-pop and music from the 2000s.
